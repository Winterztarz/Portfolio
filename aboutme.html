Photoshop, coding (and similar things)

When i was around 7 years old i enterd this horse based website called "Stallet.se" (the english version is MyDreamStable.com). The site was a place where you could "train and breed horses and be in competitions" but not really. Because what you could do on the site was design your own "stable plan" and a information page, and your own animals. So i tried painting a stable plan in paint, did not go so good, and i got bored. And it went some years before i even logged in again. But 2011 i bought my first tablet, Wacom bamboo fun pen & touch. And with that photoshop elements 8 followed. So i painted alot of painting and stable plans and things like that. And i also designed information pages. Because the information page had a picture/design as background, and then you had to code some text onto the designed picture, and maybe you had more than 1 page, then you had to link to the other pages. So a small bit of coding came in there too. And i also took my digital camera and photographed a lot and edited the pictures in photoshop. Later when i left the site behind. I started blogging about a horse game i played. So that meant editing pictures for the blog. And making headers and things for the blog design. And also playing around and coding my blog so it looked like i wanted it too, so that where i started exploring what html and css was, but never coded something completly on my own, always just editing other peoples codes they put out for free. And that is the story of why and how i started using photoshop, and coding and stuff.
